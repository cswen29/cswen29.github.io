We are the Four Eyes Band. If you love Jimi Hendrix, he is our god. We're a classical fusion psychedelic band with impaired vision. We urge everyone to see with their third and fourth eye, and we're here to elevate your journey! Experience our hit single "See into the future (kind of)"

The Band

Four Eye 1

Four Eyes One is the vocalist of the group and brings her stellar singing to the stage. Influenced by multiple unconventional genres and fusions, she represents the glue that holds the group together. She provides dedication, perseverance, and has very bad vision.

Four Eye 2

All the way from the tropics, we have our classical counterpart, known for playing the piano without looking because she claims that 'I don't need my eyes to see through you'. Her piano tunes pierces your heart and makes you a better person. She has bad vision.

Four Eye 3

Four Eyes Three plays guitar and brings the psychedelic journey to the Four Eyes Experience. Raised under the close supervision of Jimi Hendrix, Four Eyes Three is the pizazz, the spice, and also has very bad vision.
